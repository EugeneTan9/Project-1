Instructions
2 players will play against each other in the game of NIM. Each player will take turn to remove the sticks from the stacks. Each player can take any number of sticks. They are not allowed to take from another row or stacks in the same turn. They can click on the 'END TURN' button to end their turns. Whoever cicks on the last stick loses.
